Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ReuseNotes ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use reusenotes.com and the ReuseNotes web application (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Email address. ReuseNotes uses passwordless sign-in, so we store the email address you enter to identify your account and keep your notes associated with you.
  • Display name. An auto-generated name is created for your account, which you can change.
  • Your content. The notes, templates, variables, and tags you create are stored so the Service can function.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage and device data such as browser type, pages visited, and general interaction data, collected through analytics tools.
  • Cookies and similar technologies as described below.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and operate the Service.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To store and display the notes and templates you create.
  • To understand usage and improve features and performance.
  • To display advertising that helps keep the Service free (see Section 4).

3. Cookies

We use cookies and local browser storage to keep you signed in, remember your preferences (such as your notes layout), and measure usage. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect parts of the Service.

4. Google AdSense & Advertising

We may use third-party advertising, including Google AdSense, to serve ads on our public pages.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of third-party vendor cookies at aboutads.info/choices.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Service. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit, what pages they view, and what other sites they used prior to coming to our site. We use this information only to improve the Service. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. How We Store and Protect Data

Your account data and content are stored in a managed database (MongoDB Atlas). We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account information and content for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@reusenotes.com or visit our Contact page.
